Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2, 2026

AffordableStoreStuff ("we," "our," or "us") is an AI-powered e-commerce platform designed to help small business sellers create, manage, and grow their online stores. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our application and services.

By using AffordableStoreStuff, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account Information: Username, email address, and password (stored in hashed form) that you provide when creating an account.
  • Product Data: Product listings, descriptions, images, pricing, and inventory information you create or upload.
  • Order Data: Purchase history, order status, shipping details, and transaction records associated with your store or purchases.
  • AI Interaction Data: Prompts, inputs, and content generated through our AI-powered features, including product descriptions, style suggestions, and store recommendations.
  • Style Preferences: Theme selections, layout choices, and design preferences you configure for your store.
  • Cart Data: Items added to shopping carts, saved-for-later products, and related session data.
  • Usage Data: Log data such as pages visited, features used, device information, browser type, and general interaction patterns that help us improve our services.
  • Uploaded Files: Images and other files you upload for use in your store or product listings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality: To operate, maintain, and provide the core features of the platform, including account management, product listings, order processing, and store customization.
  • AI Features: To power AI-generated product descriptions, store recommendations, and content suggestions by processing your inputs through our AI services.
  • Order Processing: To facilitate transactions, process payments, and manage order fulfillment between sellers and buyers.
  • Analytics and Improvement: To analyze usage patterns, diagnose technical issues, and improve the performance and usability of our platform.
  • Communication: To send you service-related notifications, updates, and support responses.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted on secure infrastructure. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encrypted connections (TLS/SSL) for all data transmitted between your device and our servers.
  • Hashed and salted password storage -- we never store passwords in plain text.
  • Access controls that restrict data access to authorized personnel and services only.
  • Regular security reviews and updates to our infrastructure and dependencies.

While we take reasonable steps to protect your data, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following third-party services to provide our platform's functionality:

  • OpenAI: We use OpenAI's APIs to power AI-generated content features such as product descriptions and store suggestions. Data sent to OpenAI is governed by OpenAI's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Cloud Storage: Uploaded files such as product images are stored using Google Cloud infrastructure. Google Cloud's data handling is governed by Google Cloud's Privacy Notice.
  • Stripe: Payment processing is handled by Stripe. Financial information such as credit card numbers is collected and processed directly by Stripe and is not stored on our servers. Stripe's data practices are governed by Stripe's Privacy Policy.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party providers to understand how they handle your data.

5. Children's Privacy

AffordableStoreStuff is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13, we will take prompt steps to delete that information. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us at the address below.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You may request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: You may request deletion of your account and associated personal data. Certain data may be retained where required by law or for legitimate business purposes.
  • Export: You may request an export of your data in a commonly used, machine-readable format.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw that consent at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in Section 8 below. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe.
